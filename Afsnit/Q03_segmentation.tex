\qhead{3. Price Structure and Market Segmentation — How does your company segment markets? Explain the rationale and the resulting prices.}

BYD segments by willingness-to-pay using all three mechanisms of the price-structure framework \parencite{nagle2018}.

\textbf{1. Price-offer configuration (Good--Better--Best).} The brand ladder is a textbook tiered structure: Seagull (from \(\sim\)RMB~57{,}000) \(\rightarrow\) Dynasty/Ocean (below 300{,}000) \(\rightarrow\) Denza (300{,}000--500{,}000) \(\rightarrow\) Fang Cheng Bao (500{,}000--800{,}000) \(\rightarrow\) Yangwang (above 800{,}000, up to \(\sim\)RMB~21\,m). A luxury buyer will not cross-shop a Seagull, so Yangwang captures their surplus while the Seagull still serves the entry buyer — value capture across a roughly 370-fold price range.

\textbf{2. Price metric.} The DM-i story reframes the metric toward \textbf{total cost of ownership} (``electricity cheaper than oil''), aligning the charge with running-cost value rather than sticker price, which lifts willingness-to-pay.

\textbf{3. Price fences.} A \emph{product-design} fence (stripped ``Vitality'' trims without smart driving) pushes high-WTP buyers up; a \emph{versioning} fence gives ``God's Eye'' assistance free but in three tiers — C, B and A tied to the mass, flagship and Yangwang brands, differing in compute and lidar \parencite{insidechinaADAS}; a \emph{geography} fence sustains far higher prices in Europe (Question~12); and a \emph{buyer-identification} fence (the separate Yangwang badge) self-selects status buyers.

\textbf{Rationale and result.} The governing principle is that each fence must stop high-WTP buyers reaching the low price \emph{without alienating} them — and BYD satisfies it because every tier is a genuinely different offer, not an artificial restriction on the same car. A Yangwang buyer simply cannot get U8 performance in a Seagull. The result is that BYD extracts value along the whole demand curve instead of settling on a single compromise price that would either leave money on the table at the top or exclude buyers at the bottom.
